ARINA is an innovative web-based tool designed to enhance farmers experience.

This AI-powered system is designed to provide accurate weather forecasts and crop reports based on user inputs such as date, soil type, crop type, temperature, humidity, and other environmental factors. The model leverages machine learning and real-time data analytics to generate insights that help farmers and agricultural stakeholders make informed decisions about crop cultivation and management.
